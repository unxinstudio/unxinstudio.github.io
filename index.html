About Us

UNXIN STUDIO is an AI-driven audiovisual studio founded by Yunan Helmy, turning imagination and emotion into cinematic visuals.

Focused on short films and music videos, we use AI as a creative tool to express mood, feeling, and story — not just visuals. Every project is about capturing emotion and translating it into moving images.

Built on constant experimentation and adaptation, UNXIN STUDIO grows alongside fast-moving technology, pushing creative limits and aiming for global recognition through international competitions and collaborations.
